Classic Indian Summer Dessert Drink- Falooda

Falooda (also spelled Faluda) is a popular Indian summer dessert drink made with various layered elements like milk, sweet basil seeds (sabja seeds), jelly, vermicelli, ice cream, nuts, and rose syrup. Served both in restaurants and even on the streets in many Indian cities, this popular Indian dessert is perfect for warmer weather and outdoor dining.

Ingredients

  • Sabja seeds : Tiny black seeds are sweet basil seeds (tukmaria seeds). Sabja seeds are cooling to the body.
  • Rose syrup : It is a thick red colored sweet syrup made from roses, sugar and cooling as well.
  • Falooda sev : In an authentic falooda recipe, vermicelli made from corn starch or arrowroot flour is added.
  • Jelly or Jello : An optional element is jelly (jello). Add any fruity flavored vegetarian or vegan jelly
  • Milk : Make it with dairy or a vegan milk. The milk must be cold or chilled.
  • Nuts and dry fruits : Glazed cherries, tutti frutti, cashews, almonds, pistachios are some of the ingredients that you can add.
  • Ice cream : Though vanilla ice cream is preferred choice of ice cream, other ice cream varieties will also work well.

Instructions

  1. Soak ½ tablespoon Sabja Seeds (sweet basil seeds) in the water for about 20 to 30 mins.After 30 minutes, using a fine strainer drain the extra water from the fully soaked sabja seeds. Cover and keep the soaked sabja seeds aside.
  2. Meanwhile, begin to prepare the faluda sev or vermicelli according to package instructions.
  3. Then added 5 tablespoons of the vermicelli (roughly ⅓ cup).
  4. Boil in the water on medium heat until the vermicelli have softened.Once cooked, then strain them and drain the water. Rinse them in running water and set aside.
  5. Chop the nuts that you will be adding. I have added some cashews, almonds and pistachios.
  6. For serving, add the rose syrup(1.5 to 2 tablespoons ) in glasses first and then add 2 tablespoons of the soaked sabja seeds, 2 to 3 tablespoons of the falooda sev and some cubes or chunks of jelly/jello.
  7. Add about 1 cup cold or chilled milk in a slow stream taking care that the milk does not mix with the rose syrup and the sabja seeds.
  8. Finally add a scoop of ice cream.Top with sliced pistachios, golden raisins and sliced almonds. That's it! Enjoy your tasty Falooda!<
